Activities

The theme of 2018: Adventure and Devotion

UCLA - CSST program

Great team and topic on localization and prediction of prostate cancer biopsy! Appreciate the help from Dr. Corey, Karthik and other lab members. Hope to see all of you again! Thanks for CSST program. A beautiful college and wonderful place for us to live and research.

PET co-inventor Prof. Phelps

It's my honor to meet you and listen to your story. Positron Emission Computed Tomography (PET) was a great invention in human history. And it can get better if combined with more modalities. A good lecture by Prof. Phelps who is a great boxer in his whole life!

Open-lab internship - POSTECH

Amazing time spent in Korea. Lucky to have the chance of studying and reseaching in BOA Lab of Creative IT Engineering department. Appreciate the generous help of Hyojin, Sohyeon and other lab members as well as friends in this program. Look forward to seeing you guys again!

Outstanding Undergraduate Paragon Finalist

Lucky to be chosen as a candidate of outstanding undergraduate student in XJTU! Though not selected as the winners, I am highly fortunate to meet the other outstanding peers. Thanks for my family, Yamin, classmates and teachers paying close attention to me! 顽张って!

The theme of 2019: Harvest and Exploration

Japan tour - Osaka, Kyoto, Nara and Tokyo

An exhilarating trip in Japan. It is a modest and lovely country. We are lost in its beautiful scenery and tasty food. Tokyo University, Kyoto University as well as Waseda University also brought us strong university culture shock. We will be back to this miraculous island in the future.

Graduation - さよなら XJTU

There are so many memorable experiences during this four-year undergraduate program. Together we grew up in this campus and met so many great people. I can evem remember all the trails we walk and all the words we talk. XJTU is where we will always miss, recall and love forever.
